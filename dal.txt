Download our State of Sales Report 2020-2021, which contains dozens of insights
into the working lives of salespeople, sales managers and business owners,
revealing how the pandemic has impacted the way they work. 

This year, we interviewed 1,702 sales reps, sales managers, business owners and
Pipedrive customers about how they work and their sales experiences.  The
survey was conducted between November and December 2020, at the end of a year
that saw the COVID-19 pandemic have an enormous impact on our lives and the way
we work.

Three in five respondents believe that salespeople are underappreciated.
However, the majority (91%) of respondents are proud to call themselves
salespeople.
